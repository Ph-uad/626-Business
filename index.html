Mission

About

Adebouge Holdings At Adebouge Holdings, our goal is make sure every Nigerian high schooler can get a job if they completed three years of secondary education and should be able to create a job if they completed six years. by connecting digital transformation to corporate philanthropy with a special focus on underutilized market.

Adebouge Holdings seeks to leverage Bayo Adebogun's experience implementing climate change related initiatives, enabling well architected and secured digital transformation in the cloud technology space and his ability to bring together diverse groups of like-minded global stakeholders to collaborate in the business of doing good through impact investment.

What we do

  • Digital transformation

    Helping small businesses build sustainable and more secured digital infrastructure.

  • Corporate Philantropy

    Economically empowering underresourced communities through strategic partnership with established impact organizations to design and develop value education initiatives, encourage active citizenship and solve livelihood problems.
